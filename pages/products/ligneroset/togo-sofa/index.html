The Togo sofa features low, clean lines and generous polyurethane foam padding. Its deep seat and soft cushions offer incomparable comfort, inviting you to relax. Its modern, timeless look makes it a must-have for contemporary furniture.

The Togo sofa was designed in the context of the 1970s, a period when casual living lifestyle and informal aesthetics were in vogue. Michel Ducaroy, wishing to create a sofa that would match this emerging trend emerging trend, he Togo with a particular focus on freedom of movement and comfort.

As an internationally renowned contemporary design brand, Ligne Roset was quick to recognize the Togo's unique appeal. The collaboration between the designer and the brand enabled the initial concept to be perfected by adapting the materials and refining the shape of the sofa to make it as comfortable as it is aesthetically pleasing.

The Togo sofa was designed in response to the need for a very comfortable, informal sofa, offering an alternative to traditional sofas. Michel Ducaroy, the French designer, wanted to design a piece of furniture that would allow you to relax and settle into different postures without compromising comfort.

Ligne Roset's Togo sofa has become a popular choice for those seeking comfort, elegance and modernity. Its iconic design and wide range of customization options make Togo a versatile piece of furniture suitable for both private and public living spaces.
